## Purpose
SIG: Game Intelligence exists to cultivate a community of practice around intelligent techniques for games and interactive systems.

## Scope
We welcome:
- Game AI (planning, behavior trees, utility AI, search, RL)
- Player modeling and game analytics
- Procedural content generation (PCG/PCGML)
- Simulation and evaluation methodology
- Tooling and pipelines for intelligent game systems

## Operating model
- Weekly session (talks / demos / reading group)
- Quarterly workshop or mini-event
- Yearly competition and maybe publication
