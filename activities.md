## Weekly sessions
- **Reading day**: 1 paper + 1 applied take away
- **Demo day**: showcase of agent bot + improvement discussion
- **Build day**: implement a baseline agent / analytics pipeline together

## Example themes
- RL baselines for small games (gridworlds, Atari-style, Unity ML-Agents)
- Behavior trees vs utility AI in modern games
- PCG for levels, quests, and itemization
- Telemetry design and player segmentation

## How to join a session
Just show up to one of the sessions. Please check **[Events]({{ '/events/' | relative_url }})** for upcoming sessions and activities.
